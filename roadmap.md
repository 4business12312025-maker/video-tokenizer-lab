# Roadmap

## Milestone 1 - Video Reconstruction

Input: 64x64 video clip

Output: Discrete token stream

Goal:
- Convert video into tokens
- Reconstruct original video
- Measure reconstruction quality

## Milestone 2 - Token Compression

Goal:
- Measure compression ratio
- Compare token counts
- Benchmark reconstruction fidelity

## Milestone 3 - Motion Tokens

Goal:
- Separate appearance tokens from motion tokens
- Investigate more efficient video representations

## Milestone 4 - Video GPT

Goal:
- Predict future video tokens
- Generate video from token sequences
